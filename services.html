Football Analytics -
The New European Format

On December 2, Edoardo Ghezzi, FC Lugano’s data analyst, gave us an exciting look into the world of football analytics. With clear diagrams and detailed analyses, he broke down the performance of UEFA Conference League teams and showed how data plays a key role in shaping decisions in Swiss football.

Ice Hockey Analytics

On November 22, our Ice Hockey Analytics event featured Mattia Stendahl and Martin Kierot, performance coaches of the ZSC and GCK Lions. They shared insights into their work managing player load using LPS tracking on the ice and measuring progress and fitness through advanced methods like jump plate tests. The event provided a fascinating look into the data-driven techniques shaping modern hockey training.

Cycling HACK 2024: Innovating for Safer Cycling in Zurich

Cycling HACK 2024 was a great success, showcasing the innovative spirit of eight teams that pitched data-driven solutions to improve cycling safety in Zurich. Congratulations to the winners, Daylight ASAP, for their effective approach to enhancing cyclist visibility at high-risk intersections. A big thank you to all the key partners: Swiss Cycling, Eviden, Veloforum, Stadt Zürich and Kanton Zürich for their invaluable support. For more information, visit cyclinghack.ch.

Tennis Talk

On April 23rd, our club hosted Prof. Dr. Heinrich Nax for an insightful talk on game theory in tennis, with a focus on the minimax strategy. He presented fascinating studies, and we discussed the sport's evolution and the growing importance of analytics and coaching.
